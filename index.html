Bilingual: Chinese-English Audio Vocabulary

📚 欢迎来到中英声音词典！

本应用旨在为您提供一个便捷的中文与英文声音词汇学习和查询工具。我们整理了多种类别与特点的声音词汇，帮助您更好地理解和使用它们。

This application aims to provide you with a convenient tool for learning and looking up Chinese and English sound vocabulary. We have organized sound vocabulary by various categories and characteristics to help you better understand and use them.

主要功能与内容概览：

  • 🗣️ 气调与感叹词 (Interjections & Exclamations): 这类词汇主要用于表达情感、引起注意或模拟特定情景下的语气。

    These words are mainly used to express emotions, attract attention, or simulate tones in specific situations.

  • 🔊 拟声词 (Onomatopoeia): 这类词汇通过模仿自然界、人与行为、人群与环境的声音来描述事物。

    These words describe things by imitating sounds from nature, human actions, and crowds/environments.

  • 🌍 万物之声 (Sounds of All Things): 本节收录了自然界、动物、物体等各种声音的描述。

    This section includes descriptions of various sounds from nature, animals, objects, and more.

  • 🐞 昆虫拟声词 (Insect Onomatopoeia): 本节收录了不同昆虫发出的声音。

    This section collects sounds made by different insects.

  • 🗣️ 人声音的形容词汇 (Adjectives Describing Human Voices): 这里整理了描述人声音特点的形容词，按音色、音量、音调和声音特点进行分类。

    This section compiles adjectives describing human voice characteristics, categorized by timbre, volume, pitch, and other features.

每个类别都配有实时搜索功能，方便您快速查找所需词汇。希望本词典能成为您学习和探索声音词汇的得力助手！

Each category is equipped with a real-time search function, making it convenient for you to quickly find the vocabulary you need. We hope this dictionary becomes a powerful assistant in your learning and exploration of sound vocabulary!
